Engineering ML systems is not only about model accuracy. It is about shipping reliable systems that respect data constraints, scale limits, and real users.

## Three principles I follow

1. **Start with the outcome.** Optimize for decision quality, not just metrics.
2. **Design for observability.** If you cannot explain drift, you cannot fix it.
3. **Think in systems.** A model is only as good as the data and infrastructure that serve it.

This mindset is what I applied in my route optimization and data compression projects, where tradeoffs between performance, cost, and sustainability mattered.
